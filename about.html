About Us

Who We Are

Established in August 1994, Power Mac Center is the premier and leading Apple Premium Reseller in the Philippines. From our humble beginnings, we have grown to 108 locations nationwide, continuously expanding to serve emerging markets. With 29 years as a reliable retailer of Apple products and other leading accessory brands and more than 20 years as an authorized service provider of Apple products, we have become the pioneer in providing the complete Apple experience to the Filipino consumer.

What We Offer

Power Mac Center also operates Apple Authorized and Premium Service Providers that give the best repair and maintenance services. As one of the leading Apple Service Providers in the Philippines, our Apple Certified Technicians and friendly customer service officers are always ready to provide you with great advice and technical support to get you and your Mac back up and running. You can choose a service center that is most convenient and well within your reach with seven locations in Manila, and one in Cebu, Davao and Zamboanga.
